FAQ

Frequently Asked Health and Safety Questions

Where can I smoke on campus?

Atmiya college has a zero tolerance smoke free zone. Penalties apply for violators.

Where do I put the parking permit on my vehicle?

The permit owner is required to affix the permit to the vehicle wind shield in the lower corner of the driver side and the front of the vehicle in the case of two wheelers.

How long can I apply for a permit?

College staff and students may hold monthly and yearly permits, while visitors can only hold daily and hourly permits.
