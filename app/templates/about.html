About Us

Atmiya College is a major Australian university with global reach and a professional outlook. We are one of the fastest growing research colleges in the country and our courses are in very high demand.

We are an ambitious and cooperative institution which seeks to provide our students and graduates with the skills and experience they will need in the real world. We provide our 30,000 students with a unique experience and are highly regarded for our international and national accreditation for our various professional degrees.

Our internationally award winning Library is acknowledged as one of the worlds largest collection of both digital and print media. We invest heavily in collaborative research and interdisciplinary cooperation.

We also house the States largest and most efficient student, staff and guest parking systems.
